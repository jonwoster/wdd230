Scenarios for site visitors

These are the primary reasons why a person will access the site:

  • User is looking for the calendar/list of events to know when the next meeting is, agenda items, and guest speakers.
  • Seeking contact information for the chamber, incluing phone, email, and address.
  • Seeking information on what the chamber does, including mission and benefits of membership.
  • Looking for information on how to join the chamber, including cost.
  • User wanting to make a complaint against a local business and needs to be referred to some other organization or agency.

Target Personas

This site has three primary personas it is targeting:

  • Chamber Business Owners: Individuals who have a business in the area and are already members of the chamber.
  • Non-Chamber Business Owners: Individuals who have a busienss in the area but are not chamber members.
  • General Public: People who are curious about the chamber and what it does and may be confusing the chamber with a regulartory agency or Better Business Bureau.
