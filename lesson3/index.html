Site Purpose

This site has multiple purposes:

  • Act as a place where members of the chamber can see the list of upcoming events.
  • Share news about the business climate and happenings that affect businesses in the area.
  • Be an initial point of contact for people opening new businesses in the area. It is a recruiting tool.
  • Help the general public know what the chamber does and what it is for.
  • Provide contact information for related agencies and organizations such as BBB, City of Leadville, etc.
