Key Facts

  • At 10,152 feet above sea level, Leadville is the highest incorporated city in North America.
  • Population of 2613 (as of 2021).
  • Founded in 1878.
  • Median selling price for homes is $511K at $363/sqft.
  • Primary industry is tourism, with mining a close second.
  • Nearby Climax mine encompasses 14,350 acres.
