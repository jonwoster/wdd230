News Release

Trader Joes announces new location in Leadville! Trader Joes has purchased the former Family Dollar location in 2nd Street and will begin remodeling in September 2023. This will be the eigth location in Colorado and is expected to open in May 2024.

Max Chow, Regional VP of Operations, commented: "We are excited about the new location. It's a great cultural and it really fills a need for additional food and drink options in the county. I also understand that the Leadville natives are really looking forward to more snack options, especially our Orange Chicken and Peanut Butter Cups!"
