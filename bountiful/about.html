Humble Beginnings

Bountiful Foods was started by Santiago "Jim" Martinez at a card-table based roadside stand in National City in 1991. He wasn't allowed to get a permit, so he moved locations as needed to avoid fines.

His infectious smile and frequent singing became somewhat of a local legend in South San Diego County. His cousin staked him the money for his first food truck and it took off from there.

His first food truck was was parked food truck row in Chula Vista before he started going mobile using multiple food trucks. Today, Bountiful Foods is still owned by Jim & his wife Nancy.

Fast Forward

Bountiful Foods now has a modern fleet of 10 fruit-delivery vehicles, each one with low vehicle emissions.

Dispatching happens based upon GPS location of the trucks, Google-based travel predictions, and an optimization engine.
